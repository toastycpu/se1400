Competition Insight:

What is your competition doing right and wrong?

I would say, most have created a simple and easy to navigate personal and buisness website/blog makers.
But sadly most of them are paided services or monthly subscriptions. There also isn't many ways to socialize
with other webpages created by others. Depending on the serivces their websites are limited to just
the templates they have provide.

How can you do it better?

I would provided templates for elements in the blog and let them decided how to customize their layout.
Also adding elemnts to socialize with others, such as: comments, friends list,
hyperlink connections to other customized blogs, and a bit more creative liberty.
