Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

It will have the ablity to customize the web page like a blog.
You can make posts that can contain images and text.
Social aspects too maybe comment sections and top 10 friends list (hyperlinks)
A mixture between Myspace and the indie blogs of the 00's.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Since you mentioned that obtaining content from others is troublesome,
While developing I think I will use my own picture and text or copyright free items.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

I got this idea from social media not being a creative outlet for me anymore.
I wanna make a blog service for users to creativly express themselves and escape
the feeling of being stuck in the matrix

Why should a site be built for this?

The blogs that were popluar when I was a kids are long gone, overtaken by
facebook, instagram and many others. The vision that I had for it to be a fun place to be
yourself is hard with the current site builders. I can speak from experince.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Use it as a creative outlet for themselves, without the fear of being themselves.
Like an art gallery of their intrests and how they want to socialize with others.

What is the goal of the site?

A blog service that serves as an outlet for users to express themselves
and a social networking outlet without too much of the addiction.
